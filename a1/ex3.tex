\textbf{Exercise 3:} Alternative elimination rules for $\and$

Local soundness:

$$
\infer[\land E^{u,v}]
  {C\text{ true}}
  {\infer[\land I]
    {A \land B\text{ true}}
    {A\text{ true} & B\text{ true}}
  & \infer*{C}{\infer[u]{A\text{ true}}{} & \infer[u]{B\text{ true}}{}}
  }
\implies
\infer
  {C\text{ true}}
  {A\text{ true} & B\text{ true}}
$$

Local completeness:

$$
A \land B\text{ true}
\implies
\infer[\land E^{u,v}]
  {A \land B \text{ true}}
  {A \land B\text{ true}
  &\infer[\land I]
    {A \land B \text{ true}}
    {\infer[u]{A\text{ true}}{} & \infer[u]{B\text{ true}}{}}
  }
$$
